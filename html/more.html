TB Knows!
Mission Statement

"To educate, inform, and support individuals, and communities in the fight against tuberculosis by providing accurate and up-to-date information."

Vision Statement

"To become an online resource for tuberculosis education and support, empowering global communities to eradicate TB through knowledge, and collaboration."

General Purpose

The sole purpose of the website is to provide information regarding tuberculosis in the Philippines. Seeking an enhance awareness, and promote early detection, reducing the impact of tuberculosis in the country.
