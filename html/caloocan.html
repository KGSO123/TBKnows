Programs Done in Caloocan:

  1. Conduct TB screenings using X-ray services for school staff.

    Perform rapid diagnostic testing (Truenat) for TB detection.

    Enroll diagnosed individuals in appropriate treatment programs.

    Raise awareness about TB prevention and treatment within the school community.

  2. TB Caravan: Free Chest X-RAY

    This is one of the measures taken by the Caloocan City Health Department to immediately address those who need treatment for Tuberculosis. Everyone is encouraged to immediately consult a doctor if they experience symptoms such as cough, cold, fever, weight loss, spitting blood stained and possible contact with a person with TB. Look out for the arrival of the TB Caravan in your area.
